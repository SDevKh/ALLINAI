Meetings:


  • x.ai:

    • An AI assistant that helps you schedule meetings with anyone. It can handle the back-and-forth emails, find the best time and place, and send calendar invites for you.

  • Otter:

    • An AI-powered app that records and transcribes your conversations in real time. It can also generate summaries, highlights, and action items from your meetings

  • Airgram:

    • An AI tool that integrates with your video conferencing platforms and automatically records, transcribes, and indexes your meetings. It can also sync with your CRM and other tools to update your workflows.

Video:

  • Lumen5:

    • An online video maker that lets you turn your blog posts, articles, or text into engaging videos. It uses AI to match your content with relevant images, videos, and music.

  • InVideo:

    • An online video editor that lets you create professional-looking videos in minutes. It has hundreds of templates, effects, and transitions to choose from. It also has an AI feature that can automatically generate voiceovers for your videos.

  • Deepfake:

    • A technology that uses AI to manipulate faces and voices in videos. It can create realistic videos of people saying or doing things that they never did. It can also be used for entertainment, education, or research purposes.

Image Design:

  • Canva:

    • An online design platform that lets you create stunning graphics, logos, flyers, posters, and more. It has thousands of templates, icons, fonts, and images to choose from.

  • Pixlr:

    • An online photo editor that lets you edit, crop, resize, and filter your images. It also has advanced features like background removal, color splash, and AI cutout.

  • DeepArt:

    • An online tool that lets you transform your photos into artworks using the style of famous artists. It uses a deep neural network to combine the content and style of different images.
